A — Tabbed (Calendar + Coverage + Overrides)

Three tabs: Calendar (visual timeline, the main view), Coverage Wizard (find someone to cover your shift), Overrides (list + manage active overrides). Clean separation, easy to navigate. Best for users who primarily browse, then act.

B — Split-pane (Calendar + contextual action panel)

Calendar on the left, contextual action panel on the right. Click any shift → the panel shows that person's shift details with inline Override and Coverage buttons. No tab switching. Best for power users who want everything in one screen.
