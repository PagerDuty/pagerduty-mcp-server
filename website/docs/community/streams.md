# MCP Community Streams

The PagerDuty **Field Developer Engineering (FDE) Team** hosts a regular series of live streams on YouTube and Twitch dedicated to the PagerDuty MCP Server — covering new features, integrations, real-world demos, and a behind-the-scenes look at what's coming next.

## What to expect

Each stream is an informal, technical session where the FDE team:

- **Demos new tools and capabilities** as they ship — often before the release notes are published
- **Walks through real integrations** with AI clients like Cursor, VS Code, and Claude Desktop
- **Takes live questions** from the community on PagerDuty + AI workflows
- **Previews upcoming work** including tools in development and experimental branches
- **Discusses the broader MCP ecosystem** and how PagerDuty fits into the AI operations landscape

## Format

Sessions are typically 45–90 minutes. Most streams are unscripted and demo-driven — expect live terminals, real PagerDuty accounts, and occasional surprises.

Streams are recorded and archived on the [PagerDuty YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@Pagerduty/streams). Subscribe and enable notifications to catch them live.

## Community

Have a topic you'd like the team to cover? Open a discussion or drop a comment on any stream. The FDE team actively reads and responds to community input — several stream topics have come directly from GitHub Issues and viewer questions.
